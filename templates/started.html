Text Module

Text module allows you to analyze any kind of text like paragraphs, blog, speech or any article. Once you entered your text we search for keywords which represents emotions, feelings expressed through it. As a result we share a bar graph analysis of those feelings with you.







Twitter Module

Hashtags of any trending topic are useful to any twitter user. We analyze the fetched tweets based on your input hashtag & number of tweets on it for finding the feelings in them. In the result addition to bar graph analysis, we provide a pie chart showing nature of those tweets from positive to negative.









Amazon Module

One thing that every online product customer sees before buying anything are the products reviews by other users. Here in the Amazon module, a particular user's all reviews given can be fetched to perform analysis, which will give the overall opinion. You just need to put in the user id and you get the graphs. Also, cumulative analysis of every review is also possible simply by just clicking the option.
